  • About Saikaizen Academy Scholarship cum Admission Test

Saikaizen conducts Saikaizen Scholarship cum Admission Test (SSAT) before the phase commencement of a particular course. It is mandatory to qualify SSAT for admission in any of the Course, where SSAT is applicable. The students can appear in it and avail the benefit of scholarships which would be given to them in accordance with their performance in the test.
